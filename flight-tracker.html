💡 Planning Tips
🕐

Best time to book flights

HKG–MNL is a competitive short-haul route. Check Cebu Pacific, Philippine Airlines, and Cathay Pacific. Prices often drop 6–8 weeks before departure.

📅

Flexible sweet spot

Departing May 19–20 (Tue/Wed) and returning May 27–28 (Tue/Wed) tend to be cheapest. Avoid Monday/Friday peaks.

🏙️

Makati stay tips

Stay near Ayala Triangle or Legazpi Village for walkability. Grab and Angkas are cheap for transport. Check Booking.com for last-minute hotel deals.

💸

Budget estimate

Flight ~HKD 1,400–2,800 return. Airbnb ~HKD 490–900/night. Budget for 10 nights + flights: roughly HKD 7,000–12,000 total.

🌦️

May weather in Manila

Late May is the start of rainy season. Expect hot days (~33°C) with afternoon thunderstorms. Indoor malls (Greenbelt, Glorietta) are a lifesaver.
